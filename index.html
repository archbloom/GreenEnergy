Why Green Energy

We all believe that even a small savings of energy by all the devices on this planet can help of lot to keep our planet green. Everyday more and more smart devices with capabilities such as wifi are being installed and these devices allow various opportunities to save energy when they do not need to work. Taking this as the fundamental idea behind the project I thought of using honeywell APIs to achieve this motivation.
